## Proposed Working Title

**State Space Compression, ADL, and Problem Solving**

Alternate subtitle direction:

**Problem Solving as Recursive Macrostate Construction**

## Core Thesis

ADL can be read as a bounded problem-solving architecture whose central work is
not only search, prompting, or orchestration, but the repeated construction,
transformation, evaluation, and persistence of compressed cognitive
representations. In this framing, State Space Compression (SSC) provides the
cleanest mathematical and conceptual vocabulary for explaining why ADL's
cognitive stack, GHB loop, trace surfaces, memory participation, and runtime
governance fit together.

## Why This Paper Exists

The publication program now has:

- a front-door systems paper (`What Is ADL?`)
- a bounded reflective-adaptation paper (`Godel Agents and ADL`)
- a temporal/state architecture paper (`Cognitive Spacetime Manifold`)
- a mathematical intelligence-framework paper (`A Mathematical Theory of General Intelligence`)

This paper should bridge those surfaces by explaining ADL problem solving as
structured macrostate management:

- expand when the current representation is inadequate
- explore and reshape candidate structure
- compress through evaluation and selection
- persist what matters into trace, memory, and future action

That framing matters because it gives ADL a principled explanation for why
small or local models can become more effective inside a disciplined cognitive
architecture without claiming raw model superiority.

## Allowed Claims

- ADL can be interpreted as a bounded problem-solving architecture for
  recursive macrostate construction and selection.
- SSC is a useful vocabulary for explaining ADL cognition, trace, memory, and
  reasoning structure.
- GHB provides an operational pattern for expansion, exploration, and
  compression inside ADL.
- ADL problem solving depends on representation quality, evidence retention,
  evaluation, and governed persistence rather than only raw search scale.

## Claims Requiring Careful Wording

- statements that ADL improves local-model capability
- statements that SSC is the unique or final theory of problem solving
- statements that ADL already validates SSC empirically across providers
- statements comparing ADL against other agent frameworks or benchmark systems

## Claims To Exclude

- ADL solves general problem solving
- GHB is a proven optimal SSC method
- SSC alone explains all of intelligence, governance, or citizenship
- this paper empirically demonstrates model-family uplift
