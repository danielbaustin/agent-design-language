## Main Remaining Gaps

### 1. Toy examples are still the biggest hole

The manuscript needs at least one or two compact examples that make CCC and the
task-local ratio behave sensibly.

### 2. Claim labels need to be visible inside the manuscript itself

The normalized packet now distinguishes proposals, definitions, and overclaims,
but the main manuscript still needs that discipline reflected directly in
section wording.

### 3. Related work is necessary but should stay disciplined

The paper does need the Legg-Hutter, MDL, information bottleneck, and bounded
rationality comparisons. It should not get pulled into an overly broad survey.
