# Medium Article Writing Demo

Canonical command:

```bash
bash adl/tools/demo_v089_medium_article_writing.sh
```

## What It Does

This demo turns one bounded launch-essay brief into a reviewer-friendly Medium
article packet using the ADL runtime.

The packet includes:

- outline
- title and subtitle options
- article draft
- editorial notes
- publish summary
- runtime evidence

## Why It Matters

This is not a claim that ADL can run an entire publication program on its own.

It is a narrower and more truthful claim:

ADL can host a bounded writing workflow that produces a polished article packet
with explicit review surfaces, rather than leaving the result as one opaque
blob of generated prose.

## Primary Proof Surfaces

- `article_packet/draft.md`
- `article_packet/editorial_notes.md`
- `article_packet/publish_summary.json`
- `runtime/runs/v0-89-medium-article-writing/run_summary.json`
