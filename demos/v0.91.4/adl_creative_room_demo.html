Why this matters

Creativity becomes auditable without becoming dead.

The point is not to make agents less imaginative. The point is to give imagination rails: owned surfaces, explicit cuts, reviewable evidence, and a clean handoff to the next transition.

Intent packet accepted

The room starts by turning a loose creative wish into explicit deliverables, non-goals, and proof duties.

  1. Intent becomes a durable issue and prompt-card bundle.
  2. Work is split into artifact, proof, matrix, and review surfaces.
  3. The demo artifact is built inside the bounded shard.
  4. Review checks the claims against visible evidence.
  5. Closeout records what happened so the next agent inherits state.
