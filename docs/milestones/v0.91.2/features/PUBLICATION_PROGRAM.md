## Purpose

Turn the ADL arXiv and Medium publication program into reviewed source packets
and process docs, not late-stage improvised writing.

## Scope

In scope:

- arXiv and Medium publication backlog.
- Authoring process notes.
- Review gates and claim/citation discipline.
- Backlog item for "Gödel Agents and the Gödel-Hadamard-Bayes Algorithm".

Out of scope:

- Direct publication.
- Unsupported research claims.
- Marketing copy without technical evidence.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Claims, citations, speculation, and review status are separated.
- Paper/article packets are suitable for human review.
- No artifact is marked published by this milestone.
