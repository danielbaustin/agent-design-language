## Purpose

Advance the Mathematical Theory of General Intelligence source packet into a
reviewable claim/citation/review artifact without publishing or overclaiming.

## Scope

In scope:

- Claim and citation packet update.
- Unsupported-claim register.
- Review packet suitable for human handoff.
- Next authoring steps.

Out of scope:

- Public arXiv submission.
- Claims of proof, optimality, or benchmark superiority without support.
- Silent incorporation into other papers.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Every major claim has citation status or unsupported-claim status.
- Review packet preserves residual risk.
- Human reviewers can inspect the source boundary.
