# v0.91.2 Publication Program Packet

## Status

WP-13 publication-program packet for `#3012`.

## Purpose

This directory promotes the local publication planning corpus into a tracked
`v0.91.2` review surface.

The goal is not to publish papers or articles from this issue. The goal is to
leave behind one bounded packet that makes the publication program reviewable by
separating:

- backlog and sequencing,
- review gates and approval boundaries,
- article vs paper workflow expectations,
- and explicitly deferred lanes such as the Godel/GHB follow-on paper.

## Packet Scope

This packet covers:

- the bounded arXiv and Medium backlog for the current planning band,
- the repeatable review gates required before anything is treated as ready for
  public release,
- one explicit backlog note for `Godel Agents and the Godel-Hadamard-Bayes
  Algorithm`.

It does not cover:

- direct manuscript drafting,
- article drafting,
- submission or publication,
- unsupported citation or claim expansion.

## Files

- `ARXIV_AND_MEDIUM_PUBLICATION_BACKLOG_v0.91.2.md`: tracked publication
  backlog and sequencing surface.
- `PUBLICATION_REVIEW_GATES_v0.91.2.md`: review and approval gates for paper
  and article publication work.
- `GODEL_AGENTS_GHB_BACKLOG_NOTE_v0.91.2.md`: explicit backlog handling for
  the Godel/GHB paper lane.

## Truth Boundaries

- This packet is a backlog/process packet, not a publication surface.
- Local `.adl/docs/TBD/publication/` materials remain source inputs, not
  canonical milestone truth by themselves.
- No item in this packet is implicitly approved for submission or publication.
