## Overview

`v0.88` is the milestone where ADL begins treating time as a first-class substrate rather than incidental metadata.

The milestone moves the project toward:
- explicit temporal self-location
- continuity and identity grounded in ordered experience
- time-aware retrieval and future-oriented obligations
- execution-policy and cost semantics that can be reviewed alongside trace

This is not yet the full social, constitutional, or multi-agent temporal system. It is the foundation that makes those later layers possible.
