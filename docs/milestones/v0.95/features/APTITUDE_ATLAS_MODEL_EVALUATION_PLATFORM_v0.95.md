# v0.95 Feature: Capability-Testing Evidence And Aptitude Atlas Boundary

## Status

Forward-planning feature contract for `v0.95`.

Aptitude Atlas productization is post-`v0.95`. The MVP consumes tracked
capability-testing evidence only.

## Purpose

Define how `v0.95` consumes model capability-testing evidence while keeping
Aptitude Atlas product work outside the MVP.

## Source Inputs

- `docs/planning/ADL_FEATURE_LIST.md`
- `docs/GLOSSARY.md`
- `docs/milestones/v0.90.1/ideas/ANRM_SCAFFOLDED_SMALL_MODEL_EXPERIMENT.md`
- `docs/milestones/v0.85/features/ROAD_TO_v0.95.md`

## Scope

This feature should establish:

- the tracked capability-testing evidence that MVP review may consume
- the relationship between capability harnesses, aptitude families, provider
  suitability, and reviewer-facing evidence
- a bounded MVP evidence surface for model comparison that remains
  methodology-backed
- the post-`v0.95` boundary for Aptitude Atlas productization

## Non-goals

- Aptitude Atlas productization or public baseline-product launch; both are
  post-`v0.95`
- a vague leaderboard without reviewable methodology
- collapsing all cognitive or provider quality into one mythology score
- claiming broad product maturity from the `v0.91.1` harness alone

## Completion Target

`v0.95` evidence-consumption boundary. Aptitude Atlas productization is
post-`v0.95`.
