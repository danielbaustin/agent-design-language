# Vision - v0.91

## Moral Governance, Wellbeing, And Cognitive-Being Foundations

v0.91 is the milestone where ADL should begin turning moral and cognitive-being
vocabulary into reviewable runtime evidence.

The aim is not to declare that ADL has solved morality. The aim is to make
morally significant choices legible: what happened, what alternatives were
available, what was refused, what evidence was used, what outcomes followed, and
how the trajectory should be reviewed over time.

## Strategic Thesis

The CSM polis cannot safely move toward birthday, citizenship, reputation,
Theory of Mind, or constitutional review unless it first has moral trace,
wellbeing, secure communication, and cognitive-being evidence. v0.91 supplies
that foundation while preserving v0.92 as the identity and birthday milestone.

The milestone should make these ideas concrete:

- Freedom Gate moral events are durable evidence.
- Moral trace links choices, alternatives, reasons, outcomes, attribution, and
  review.
- Moral metrics are signals for reflection, not scalar moral verdicts.
- Wellbeing is a decomposable diagnostic tied to reality contact, continuity,
  agency, progress, moral integrity, and participation.
- Kindness is inspectable support and non-harm under conflict, not merely a nice
  tone.
- Humor and absurdity are bounded frame-detection and reframing surfaces.
- Affect-like signals are explicit reasoning-control evidence, not claims of
  hidden emotion.
- Cultivated intelligence requires formation, restraint, reasonableness, and
  moral participation.
- Anti-harm constraints must detect harmful trajectories, including decomposed
  and delegated harm.
- Moral resources should support care, refusal, attention, and
  anti-dehumanization.
- Agent communication must be local, secure, identity-bound, traceable, and
  redacted before it can support stronger identity or birthday claims.

## What Success Looks Like

A reviewer can inspect a bounded proof packet and see:

- a moral event emitted at a Freedom Gate crossing
- the selected action and rejected alternatives
- a validation result for the event record
- an outcome-linkage record with uncertainty preserved
- moral metrics derived from trace evidence
- a trajectory review that explains trends without collapsing them into a score
- a wellbeing diagnostic with policy-bound visibility
- an anti-harm denial or refusal event for a synthetic delegated-harm scenario
- kindness, reframing, affect, and moral-resource records linked to trace
- a secure intra-polis agent communication or invocation record that does not
  depend on external transport

## What This Enables

v0.91 prepares ADL for:

- v0.92 identity, continuity, memory grounding, and the first true birthday
- a v0.91.1 adjacent-systems lane for capability/aptitude testing,
  intelligence metric architecture, ANRM/Gemma, ToM, memory/identity,
  runtime-v2/polis docs, and any ACIP hardening
- v0.93 constitutional citizenship, social cognition, reputation, and shared
  social memory
- safer citizen-facing Observatory surfaces
- reviewable moral learning without ungrounded self-improvement claims

## What It Must Avoid

v0.91 must not claim final moral agency, consciousness, legal personhood,
constitutional authority, or a solved theory of wellbeing. It must not turn
karma, wellbeing, or moral metrics into a public scoreboard. It must not expose
private wellbeing diagnostics as reputation. It must not move the first birthday
or durable identity architecture out of v0.92. It must not implement external
agent communication without TLS or mutual-TLS-equivalent protection.
