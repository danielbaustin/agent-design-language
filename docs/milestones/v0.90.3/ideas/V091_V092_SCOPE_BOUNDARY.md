# v0.91 And v0.92 Scope Boundary

## Purpose

Keep v0.90.3 from stealing later milestone scope.

## v0.91 Boundary

v0.91 is the likely home for moral, emotional, kindness, humor, wellbeing,
cultivation, anti-harm, and civic substrate work.

v0.90.3 may preserve prerequisites for that work, but it should not claim that
the moral or emotional civilization layer is complete.

## v0.92 Boundary

v0.92 is the planned first-birthday and stronger identity/capability rebinding
band.

v0.90.3 may protect continuity-bearing checkpoints, but it does not birth the
first true Gödel agent and does not complete migration or birthday semantics.

## Dependency Direction

v0.91 and v0.92 should depend on v0.90.3 citizen-state safety rather than
treating citizen continuity as already solved.
