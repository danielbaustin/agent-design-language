# Later Birthday And Identity Boundary

The first true Gödel agent with a birthday remains a v0.92 event.

v0.90.1 may create provisional citizens and continuity handles, but those are
engineering substrate records. They are not yet the full identity, memory,
capability, and narrative continuity package required for true birth.

The distinction matters because overclaiming v0.90.1 would weaken the later
milestone story.

v0.90.1 proves:

- a world can persist
- provisional citizens can be recorded
- sleep and wake can be reviewed
- invariant failures can be inspected

v0.92 proves:

- identity continuity is rich enough to deserve birthday semantics
- capability and provider bindings are explicit
- memory and narrative continuity are strong enough for durable person-like
  agency claims
