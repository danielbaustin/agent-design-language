# Payment And Inter-Polis Deferral

v0.90.4 should prove contracts before payment.

Payment rails are attractive, but they introduce legal, accounting, trust,
settlement, fraud, identity, and jurisdictional concerns that are larger than
the first contract-market substrate.

## Deferred Areas

- Lightning
- x402
- stablecoins
- bank settlement
- invoices
- taxation
- production legal contracts
- inter-polis exchange rates
- cross-polis reputation markets

## Why Defer

A payment system without trustworthy contract authority would make the polis
more dangerous, not more capable. The safer order is:

1. define governed contracts and bids
2. prove lifecycle and transition authority
3. prove delegation and review summaries
4. decide reputation/resource accounting
5. only then consider payment settlement
