# v0.90.3 Citizen-State Dependency

v0.90.4 depends on v0.90.3 because economic agency needs safe standing.

Contracts, bids, delegation, and external counterparty participation all depend
on knowing who may act, who may inspect, who may accept obligations, and which
actions are mediated through governed identity.

v0.90.4 should consume:

- citizen and guest standing
- service actor boundaries
- human provider and CSM identity distinctions
- access-control semantics
- projection policy
- challenge and appeal flow
- quarantine and sanctuary behavior
- continuity witnesses and receipts

If those surfaces are incomplete when v0.90.4 starts, the milestone should use
fixtures and explicit non-claims rather than redefining the missing doctrine.
